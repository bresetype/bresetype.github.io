Typefaces About

I’m Alex — a Swiss type designer, font developer, and typographer.

I’ve been working in the world of print, typography, and type design for over two decades.
Along the way, I’ve developed a strong focus on type history and design education, sharing what I know through teaching and workshops at places like ECAL Master Type Design (CH), SFGZ (CH), Winchester School of Art (UK).
Whether it’s beginners or advanced students, I love helping others explore the art and craft of type.

From 2012 to 2024, I was part of the team at Dalton Maag in London, working as a type designer, font developer, and team manager. I had the chance to help shape the visual identity of global brands like Amazon, DHL, Facebook, FedEx, HP, Nokia, and Oracle — designing custom, multi-script typefaces, refining logotypes and wordmarks, and creating fonts used by millions around the world.

Beyond design work, I'm also helped train new team members, built internal knowledge systems, and worked on improving how we develop fonts — making the whole process smoother and more efficient.
