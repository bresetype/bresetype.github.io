From 2012 to 2024, I worked as a font developer and team manager at Dalton Maag, where I helped shape the visual language and type systems of some of the world’s most recognized brands, including Amazon, DHL, Facebook, FedEx, HP, Nokia, and Oracle. I’ve developed custom, multi-script typefaces that reach readers and users globally. I also designed and refined logotypes, wordmarks and worked on font modifications.
