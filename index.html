Alex Blattmann

Hi, I’m Alex Blattmann — a Swiss type designer, font developer, and typographer.

With over two decades of experience in Print, Typography, and Type Design, I’ve developed a deep passion for education in typography and type, building knowledge bases, and optimizing modern font development workflows.

From 2012 to 2024, I worked as a font developer and team manager at Dalton Maag, where I helped shape the visual language and type systems of some of the world’s most recognized brands, including Amazon, DHL, Facebook, FedEx, HP, Nokia, and Oracle. I’ve developed custom, multi-script typefaces that reach readers and users globally.
